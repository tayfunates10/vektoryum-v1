# F99-8 Final Fidelity Gate

This phase defines the final fail-closed publication gate for Fidelity-99.

## Acceptance thresholds

- Every qualified artifact must have an overall fidelity score of at least `0.990`.
- Geometric, edge, color, typography and print-readiness component scores must each be at least `0.980`.
- Any artifact below the overall threshold is rejected and must not be published.
- Source and output evidence must be bound to immutable SHA-256 digests.
- Missing components, duplicate cases, invalid digests, non-finite values, publication of rejected artifacts or corpus shrinkage fails closed.

The qualified corpus contains at least 24 uniquely identified artifacts and does not permit aggregate scores to hide weak cases.
