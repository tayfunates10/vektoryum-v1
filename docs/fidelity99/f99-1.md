# F99-1 — Geometric fidelity contract

F99-1 defines the first finite Fidelity-99 release gate. It does not claim universal perceptual accuracy or completion of F99-2 through F99-8.

Mandatory gates:

1. Qualified corpus size cannot shrink below 8 immutable cases.
2. Mean IoU must be at least 0.990.
3. The fifth-percentile IoU must be at least 0.980.
4. Normalized symmetric Hausdorff distance must be at most 0.005 for every case.
5. Topology mismatch count must be zero for every case.
6. Missing, NaN, infinite, out-of-range or duplicate measurements fail closed.

A single weighted percentage cannot override a failed finite gate. Later phases may raise thresholds but may not weaken these rules.
