# F99-3 — Finite color fidelity contract

F99-3 qualifies color preservation independently from geometry and curves.

## Required evidence

Every qualified corpus case must provide a unique finite record containing:

- `perceptual_color_score` in `[0, 1]`;
- `normalized_delta_e` in `[0, 1]`;
- integer `palette_omissions`, `palette_duplicates`, and `transparency_mismatches` greater than or equal to zero.

The corpus is rejected when case identifiers are missing or duplicated, evidence shrinks below the declared minimum, or any value is absent, non-finite, or outside its range.

## Release thresholds

- mean perceptual color score `>= 0.990`;
- p05 perceptual color score `>= 0.980`;
- p95 normalized Delta E `<= 0.020`;
- palette omissions, palette duplicates, and transparency mismatches are all exactly zero.

F99-3 does not mark later Fidelity-99 phases complete.
