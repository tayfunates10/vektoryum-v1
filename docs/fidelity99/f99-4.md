# F99-4 Typography and Fine Detail

This phase defines a finite, fail-closed acceptance contract for text, glyph counters, narrow strokes and small decorative details.

## Qualified evidence

The contract requires at least 20 uniquely named cases spanning small text, condensed type, serif details, script-like strokes, outlined type, counters, punctuation and fine-line emblems.

## Acceptance thresholds

- Mean glyph fidelity score: at least `0.990`.
- p05 glyph fidelity score: at least `0.980`.
- p95 normalized stroke-width error: at most `0.020`.
- Missing glyphs: `0`.
- Merged glyphs: `0`.
- Broken counters: `0`.
- Lost fine details: `0`.

Missing identifiers, duplicate cases, non-finite or out-of-range metrics, negative defect counts, corpus shrinkage, or any threshold regression fails closed.
