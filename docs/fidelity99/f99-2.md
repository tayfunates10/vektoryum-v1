# F99-2 — Edge and curve quality

F99-2 is a finite, fail-closed release contract for edge continuity and curve quality.

## Acceptance criteria

A qualified corpus is accepted only when all conditions hold:

- at least 24 unique cases are present;
- mean edge F-score is at least `0.990`;
- p05 edge F-score is at least `0.980`;
- p95 normalized tangent error is at most `0.020`;
- p95 normalized curvature error is at most `0.030`;
- open-contour, self-intersection and cusp-regression counts are all zero;
- every case contains finite, bounded metrics and a unique stable id;
- corpus shrinkage, duplicate ids, missing evidence, NaN/Infinity or threshold regression fails closed.

This phase does not claim F99-3 through F99-8 completion.
